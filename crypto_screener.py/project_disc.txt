This project is a live crypto scanner that uses ATR ,RSI and RSI based SMA to find cryptocurrencies with high volitility and predict the
reversal points allowing traders and investors to make better more informed descions about their trading and acts as a screener showing
only meaningful and simple content to the traders abstracting all the complexities of the various algorithms

ATR: Average True Range a measure of volitility of a commodity developed by J. Welles Wilder, Jr.
RSI: Relative strength index (RSI) is a momentum indicator used in technical analysis to predict times/zones with highest probablity of reversals

The second part of this project is a ATR scanner that displays crypto with high volitility
